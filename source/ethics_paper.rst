Ethics Paper (CPRE/EE 394)
==========================

How to Add Ethics Paper PDF
---------------------------

1. Place your PDF at ``source/_static/pdfs/Ethics_paper.pdf``.
2. Rebuild docs with ``build-pages.bat``.
